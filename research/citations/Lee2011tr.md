# Lee.2011tr — Weight Trimming and Propensity Score Weighting

Lee, B. K., Lessler, J., & Stuart, E. A. (2011). Weight Trimming and Propensity
Score Weighting. *PLoS ONE*, 6(3), e18174. DOI: 10.1371/journal.pone.0018174

**Verified 2026-07-18:** fetched PLOS landing page; all details confirmed.

**Key finding:** trimming improves IPW under misspecified logistic regression
but did NOT improve boosted CART or random forest performance in any scenario;
authors recommend improving PS model specification over post-hoc trimming.

**Used for:** Methods (justifies the 1st/99th percentile trimming sensitivity)
and Discussion (frame for interpreting whether v2 trimming results replicate
their logit-benefits-most pattern).
